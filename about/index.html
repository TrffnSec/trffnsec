Triffin Augustine

AKA TrffnSec

My name is Triffin Augustine but most people online know me as TrffnSec. I’m a hacker, content creator, keynote speaker, help organize conferences, and teach people how to hack! Before becoming a content creator, I was the head of Hacker Education at HackerOne. I have been hacking since I was a teenager but my professional career as an ethical hacker and bug bounty hunter didn’t start until 2014. I have helped identify and exploit thousands of security vulnerabilities across hundreds of web and mobile applications for companies such as Amazon, Apple, Google, Airbnb, Snapchat, The US Department of Defense, Zoom, and more. You may have also seen me on YouTube or Twitch, where I create content to help others get into ethical hacking, bug bounty and reconnaissance. I also love playing video games like Watchdogs, GTA V, PUBG, R6 Siege, and Call of Duty Warzone!
